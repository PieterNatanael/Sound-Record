Low Battery Voice Recorder

A minimal voice recorder designed to save battery. Record in low, medium, or high quality, dim the screen to near-zero brightness, and manage your recordings with playlist and loop playback.

Privacy Policy

Low Battery Voice Recorder does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- All recordings are stored locally on your device only.
- No analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind.
- No account or login required.
- No data is ever sent to any server.

Your recordings and all activity within the app remain solely on your device.
